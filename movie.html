About Damsel Movie

“Damsel” follows Elodie, played by Millie Bobby Brown, a princess of a poverty-stricken kingdom on the decline. Her father marries her off to a prince in a different kingdom to secure funds for their homeland. After some unforeseeable circumstances, the movie turns into a monster movie.
